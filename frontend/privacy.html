Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ClearView.ai ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Using

We believe in privacy by default. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Video Data: When you submit a video link for watermark removal, our system temporarily processes the video. The original and processed files are stored on our servers for a maximum of 2 hours to allow you to download them. After this period, they are permanently and automatically deleted.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information (such as email, name, or phone number) to use our service.
  • Logs: Like most websites, we may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, time) for security and debugging purposes. These logs are rotated regularly.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve our service. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt-out of this by using browser extensions or settings.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website processes content from Meta AI. We are an independent tool and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Meta Platforms, Inc. Use of Meta's services is subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our contact form.
